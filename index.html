How Much Do You Get Paid On TikTok In 2023?

TikTok Creator Fund

One of the best ways to earn from TikTok is to join “TikTok’s Creator Fund”.

It is officially funded by TikTok to reward creators for their content. 

To get paid from TikTok, you will have to apply for the creator’s fund and if you meet its criteria, you’ll get paid per 1000 views. 

All the TikTok creators who want to make money on the platform will need at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days to be eligible to join the Creator Fund. 

Are there any eligibility criteria to join the Creator Fund?

Yes, if you’re a TikTok video creator, you need the following things to be eligible to apply to the Creator Fund:

  • You should be from the US, UK, France, Germany, Spain, or Italy
  • At least 18 years old
  • Have at least 10,000 followers
  • Have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days

As you can see, this program is NOT for everyone. Also, TikTok is banned in many leading countries like India.

How to apply for TikTok Creator Fund?

You can apply for the creator fund directly through the TikTok app. 

Here’s a quick tutorial for you.

  • Open the TikTok app 
  • Go to your Creator or Pro Account in the Settings menu
  • Next, click on ‘TikTok Creator Fund’  (If you meet the eligibility criteria, you’ll see an option to apply)

Once your account is approved you can start earning depending on the views.
